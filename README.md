# **CarbonWealth: Target-Driven Sustainability**
CarbonWealth is a web-based platform that empowers users to turn environmental impact into financial targets. By setting a monthly goal, users receive a personalized roadmap of green actions that generate carbon credits, bridging the gap between ecological responsibility and financial incentive.
# **Features**
## 1. Personalized Onboarding
  ### Secure Authentication: User registration and login system to protect personal data and track long-term progress.
  ### Lifestyle Survey: A one-time personalized assessment of living habits, housing, and transportation to ensure recommendations are realistic and high-impact.
## 2. Goal-Oriented Dashboard
  ### Target Setting: Users define a specific monthly amount (e.g., Rs.500) they wish to achieve through carbon-offsetting activities.
  ### Dynamic Recommendations: An intelligent engine suggests specific actions (e.g., energy efficiency upgrades, waste reduction) tailored to the user's survey data and financial target.
## 3. Progress Tracking
  ### Impact Visualizer: A dedicated progress page that tracks the completion of actions against the monthly target.
  ### Conversion Logic: Clear transparency on how ecological actions translate into carbon credit value and, ultimately, the user's target currency.
# **Technical Workflow**
### The application operates on a four-step logic flow:
### Data Collection: Captures user baseline via the survey.
### Conversion Engine: Calculates the Carbon Dioxide Equivalent of various activities and maps them to current carbon market values.
### Optimization Algorithm: Matches a combination of low, medium, and high-effort tasks to reach the user's specific Rupees Target Amount.
### Verification: Users log completed tasks to update their progress bar.
# **Tech Stack (Suggested/Common)**
### Frontend: Vue.js 
### Backend: Node.js / Python (FlaskAPI)
### Database: SQLite
### Styling: CSS
# **How to Use**
### Sign Up: Create an account and verify your email.
### Survey: Complete the lifestyle survey.
### Set Target: Enter your desired monthly carbon credit earnings on the Main Page.
### Act: Choose from the list of recommended actions.
### Track: Visit the Progress page to see how close you are to your goal.
